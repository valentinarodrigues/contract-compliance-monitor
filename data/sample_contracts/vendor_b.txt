SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT — CLOUD OBJECT STORAGE

1. DEFINITIONS
   "Storage Service" means the CloudVault managed object storage platform.
   "Availability" means the ability to successfully store and retrieve objects.
   "Data Transfer" means the total volume of data read from or written to the Storage Service.

2. AVAILABILITY
   2.1 CloudVault Ltd. guarantees Storage Service availability of 99.9% per calendar month.
   2.2 Availability is measured by automated health checks every 5 minutes.
   2.3 Failures to meet the 99.9% SLA entitle Client to a pro-rated credit as follows:
       - 99.0%–99.9%: 10% credit
       - 95.0%–99.0%: 25% credit
       - Below 95.0%: 50% credit

3. PERFORMANCE
   3.1 Latency: Median (P50) latency for object operations shall not exceed 100 milliseconds
       per day, measured across all API calls.
   3.2 Throughput: The service shall sustain a minimum of 1,000 concurrent requests per second.

4. USAGE CAPS
   4.1 API Calls: Client's total API calls shall not exceed 50,000 per calendar day.
   4.2 Data Transfer: Total daily data transfer (inbound + outbound) shall not exceed
       1,000 GB (1 TB) per day without a separate bandwidth agreement.
   4.3 Overage for data transfer is billed at $0.05 per GB above the daily cap.

5. SECURITY
   5.1 All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
   5.2 All data in transit uses TLS 1.2 or higher.

6. SUPPORT
   6.1 Critical incidents are responded to within 1 hour.
   6.2 Standard incidents are responded to within 4 business hours.

7. AUDIT RIGHTS
   7.1 Client may request usage and compliance reports quarterly.
   7.2 CloudVault Ltd. will provide reports within 10 business days of request.
